Beef Stirfry

Description

Stir Fry (Stir frying) is an ancient technique of frying a small amount of hot oil and stirring it into a wok. This phenomenon origniated in China and in recent passings of 100 year amounts, has spread to other regions of the orient and West. It is similar to sauteing in Western cooking techniques.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vege oil
  • 1 pound beef sirloin, 2 inch strips
  • 1 1/2/ cups of fresh broccoli heads
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into matchsticks
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted

Directions

The most time consuming part of stir fry is the prep: chopping. Slicing the meats and vegetables into smaller sized pieces can be time consuming. After chopping place the beef on one side of the wok or frying pan and the veggies on the other. Once they are cooked you can mix the two sides together.

  1. Heat veggie oil in wok or skillet over medium-high heat
  2. cook and stir beef until browned for 3-4 minutes
  3. move beef aside in wok and add brocolli, bell pepper, carrots, green onion, and garlic to center of wok
  4. cook and stir vegetables for 2 minutes
  5. stir beef into vegetables and season with soy sauce and sesame seeds
  6. continue to cook and stir vegetables are tender, for 2 minutes
