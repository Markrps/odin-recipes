Chicken Pot Pie

Description

Chicken pot pie is a North American term for a meat pie based dish topped with pie crust. they contain but are not limited to poultry, beef, seafood, or plant-based meat substitutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken, skinless/boneless cut into coubes
  • Vegetables: peas, carrots, onions, and celerey. Additionally if you are daring you may ad mushrooms or green peppers.
  • Butter and flour. This dish calls for filling, these are needed for filling.
  • Seasoning. In order to form a more perfect dish: salt, pepper, and celery seed may be used.
  • Broth and milk. Chicken broth and milk add a depth of flavor for texture to smooth our the filling consistency.
  • Pie crusts. 2 are needed.

Directions

  1. Boil the cubed chicekn with the carrots, peas, and celery for about 15 minutes.
  2. Drain and set aside contents.
  3. Cook the onions in butter until translucent
  4. Stir flour and seasonings
  5. Add chicken broth and milk simmer until filling turns thick
  6. Fill and bake the pie
  7. Place chicken and vegetable mixture in unbaked pie crust
  8. Pour chicken broth mixture over it.
  9. Cover with top crust, seal the edges, and cut slits in top to allow for vapor heat to excape
  10. Bake pastry to golden brown hue and filling is bubbly
